This Month

DECEMBER

The last playlist for the year - we start things off at a slower tempo with Yusseff Kamaal 'Yo Chavez' and Knox Fortune 'Lil Thing' from his well received debut, Paradise. George Fitzgerald's latest release 'Burns' pushes further into the dancefloor cuts, before we peak with Dusky's Detroit-nodding club track 'LF10'. To round off we finsh with a classic from Kerri Chandler.

Last Month

Kicking off with Bok Bok's edit of Kelela's "Truth Or Dare", which dubs over Steve Gurley's "Hotboyz". From garage, we move into a choppy mix of grime and heavy-hitting house cuts. Dusky's influence ruled over this month's listening with a number of their recent drops.
